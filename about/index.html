Online Learning Design Concept

About Acdmy

Acdmy is a design concept. It is an outpouring of ideas; an outlet of pent up frustration. It is my take on what a trully good synchronous online learning platform should look like if it were designed by educators, for educators. Though it is envisioned as a way for a group of people to meet online and communicate/collaborate in real time using video, audio, text, etc., Acdmy is not a web conferencing platform. It is an online learning platform, meant for students and teachers.

Acdmy is imagined to be different. It is an online environment that is conducive to student interaction in both formal and informal ways. It is built on concepts that are grounded in educational theory and the learning science to promote social and emotional presence, practical inquiry, motivation, active learning, and personalized learning. It reduces cognitive load and streamlines best practices in teaching and learning.

Why create Acdmy?

Because learning shouldn't play second fiddle to anyone. Adobe Connect and Zoom and Blackboard Collaborate and _newrow and WebEx and every other web conferencing platform on the market today are not designed for education (even the ones that claim they are). Their core features are not what educators need, want, or use. The money is in corporate clients, but our future is in students. Acdmy wants to put educators first.

Will Acdmy become a real product? None of the ideas here are real. They are mockups and concepts. They are dreams of a better synchronous learning future. Please explore the ideas and contribute dreams of your own! Maybe we can make this reality together.
